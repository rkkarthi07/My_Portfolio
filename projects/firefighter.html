Fire fighting Robot using Ardiuno Uno

  • Components :

    Arduino UNO, SIM 800l, Flame sensor, MQ2 sensor, L293 Motor driver, Relay , Servo motor, DC Water pump, DC Motor

  • Github URL: Project Link

The fire-fighting robot, driven by an Arduino UNO and an array of sensors, stands as a beacon of safety in hazardous environments. Equipped with a SIM 800l module, it communicates remotely for real-time updates. The flame sensor acts as the vigilant sentinel, detecting fires in their infancy, while the MQ2 and MQ3 sensors provide an extra layer of safety by detecting harmful gases.

Aided by an L293 Motor driver and BO Motors with sturdy wheels, the robot navigates obstacles with finesse. The relay module ensures a steady water supply to combat flames effectively. This technological sentinel, with its precision firefighting capabilities, stands ready to protect lives and property, combining ingenuity and engineering in the battle against fires.
